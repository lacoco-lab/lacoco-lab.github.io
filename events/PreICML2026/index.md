# ELLIS Sites ICML Fest 2026 at Saarbrücken, 2026

## Information

WHEN: June 30, 2026, 10:00-13:00

WHERE: [Building C7.4 at Saarland University Campus](https://www.uni-saarland.de/standort/saarbruecken/lageplan/gebaeude/c74.html), Conference Room 1.17

This event is part of the [ELLIS Sites ICML Fest 2026: Celebrate, Connect, Collaborate](https://ellis.eu/events/ellis-sites-icml-fest-2026), associated with [our ELLIS unit](https://www.ellis-unit-sam.de/).
